Bio

My name is Courtney Ward. I am a software engineer and developer based in Houston, Texas, USA. I have a degree in computer science with experience in web, game, & application development and with writing database queries.

I am passionate about software design and development. I was introduced to coding while on a different career path. I have always loved solving problems and being creative. I never imagined finding a way to integrate these two loves. Yet, in discovering software design & development, I found just that. I was sold. From that moment on, I knew programming was my future.
